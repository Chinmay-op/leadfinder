# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Internal team members at our AI services company (finding mid-size to enterprise clients for AI automation).

## Product Purpose
Discover, score, and enrich potential client leads using Gemini to extract requirements and Apollo.io for contact data. Success is finding high-fit, reachable companies to pitch our AI solutions to.

## Brand Personality
Expert, Restrained, Technical

## Anti-references
- No standard flashy glassmorphism.
- No generic AI SaaS tropes (e.g. tiny uppercase eyebrows, dark mode default without intent).

## Design Principles
- Data-dense and highly legible
- Professional and restrained (function over form)
- Clear visual hierarchy prioritizing fit scores and contact data

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict ≥4.5:1 text contrast
- Avoid solely color-coding for status (use text + color)
- Support `prefers-reduced-motion`
